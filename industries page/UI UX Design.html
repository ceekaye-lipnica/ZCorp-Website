Our Process

Empathize—Research Users' Needs

  • Dive into user-centric research to gain empathic understanding.

  • Consult experts, conduct observations, and immerse in users' environments for deeper insights.

Define—State Users' Needs and Problems

  • Organize gathered information from the Empathize stage.

  • Define core problems in a human-centered manner, focusing on users' needs and problems.

Ideate—Unleashing Creativity

  • Generate ideas from different perspectives.

  • Utilize ideation techniques like Brainstorm and SCAMPER to explore innovative solutions.

Prototype—Create Solutions

  • Produce scaled-down versions of product features for testing.

  • Experiment with prototypes to identify the best possible solutions, iteratively refining based on user experiences.

Test—Try Solutions Out

  • Test prototypes to derive a deep understanding of the product and users.

  • Investigate, accept, improve, or reject solutions based on real user interactions and experiences.
